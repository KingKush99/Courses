DNA — In a Nutshell

What is DNA? A double helix that stores the recipes of life. Letters: A, T, C, G.

  • Structure: Two strands, A–T and C–G base pairing, sugar‑phosphate backbone.
  • Replication: Helicase unzips, DNA polymerase copies, ligase joins.
  • Central Dogma: DNA → RNA → Protein.
  • Tools: PCR, gels, CRISPR.
